About Us

Aman General Trading, Shipping & Customs Clearance

Aman International for Logistics Services, Sea and Air Freight and Customs Clearance was established in 2022 as an integrated commercial services company (import, freight, clearance) in the State of Kuwait, which is the result of the merger of two well-established companies in the market working in a spirit of responsibility and applying the highest levels of seriousness, seeking to gain customer satisfaction and build trust Mutual through its accumulated experiences at the knowledge and professional level, where it is honored to serve its clients in its branches located in both China and Kuwait.
Aman Company for General Trading, Shipping and Customs Clearance offers various services. It is fully confident in the ability to deal and contain situations, whatever their difficulty, and to develop appropriate solutions and strategies for all customers' problems and challenges that may face them.

  • Effective solutions exist to support clients' financial and business goals.
  • Forming successful partnerships with clients.
  • Tracking the goods from purchase, then shipment, until receiving the goods safely.

Air freight services

For all major airlines, we offer a complete list of air logistics solutions for shipments globally and for a variety of industries.

Sea freight services

We ship with all types of land freight including 20ft, 40ft, 40HQ containers as well as LCL consolidated freight. Offering competitive rates and providing options for your needs.

Customs clearance services

Providing clearance services at the ports and ensuring the availability of the required papers, as we guarantee you to carry out the customs clearance process efficiently and quickly.
